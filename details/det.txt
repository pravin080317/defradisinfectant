## ‚úÖ Solution: Use `aria-labelledby` on the `<input>` referencing the `<td>` containing the filename

Here‚Äôs how to do it:

---

### üîç Step-by-step Fix (without merging columns)

#### ‚úÖ 1. Assign an `id` to the `<td>` that holds the file name

```html

#### ‚úÖ 2. Use `aria-labelledby` on the checkbox input

  id="select-schedule-{{ row-index }}"

  value="{{ result.voap_filename }}+{{ result.voap_filepath }}"

```

Now, the checkbox is *programmatically associated* with the filename, even though it‚Äôs in another cell.

## ‚úÖ Why This Works

- `aria-labelledby` links the checkbox to the filename cell ‚Äî screen readers will read the filename when focusing the checkbox.
- You keep your existing visual layout ‚Äî checkbox column stays separate from the filename.
- It passes **WCAG 2.2 Success Criteria 1.3.1 and 3.3.2**, as it clearly defines the control‚Äôs purpose and label.
