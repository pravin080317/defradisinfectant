Reusable components (e.g., C# plugins, Azure Functions) are modularised and stored in shared Azure DevOps repos, with a plan to open source elements where appropriate.

REST APIs follow OpenAPI standards; frontend uses Gov.UK Design System.

Hosted on Microsoft Power Platform and Azure, both widely adopted government platforms.

Schedule data and user actions are logged in Dataverse and may be published in a structured, open format subject to governance.
